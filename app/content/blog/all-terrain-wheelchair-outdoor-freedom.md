# All-Terrain Wheelchair UK: Beach, Forest, Gravel and Stairs in One Chair

Most UK power wheelchair users own (or wish they owned) more than one chair: a lightweight indoor model, a rugged outdoor model, and possibly a beach or trail attachment. The **XSTO X12 Pro** is designed to replace all of them — including the stairlift — with a single **all-terrain stair climbing wheelchair**.

## What "All-Terrain" Actually Means

Not every chair marketed as "off-road" lives up to the name. The X12 Pro's specifications are built around real-world UK conditions:

- **40° stair climbing** — domestic and public stairs
- **300 mm ditch crossing** in track mode
- **220 mm rear obstacle** and **100 mm forward obstacle** clearance
- **IPX5 weather resistance** — rain, splash, light hose-down
- **35 km range** on dual batteries — full-day excursions
- **136 kg load capacity**

## Where Owners Take It

- **Beaches** — track mode handles dry and packed sand
- **Forest trails** — independent suspension and track grip on roots and mud
- **Gravel driveways and country lanes**
- **Cobbled streets** in historic UK towns
- **Grass verges, fields, and parkland**
- **Kerbs and unadapted pavements**
- **Stairs** in shops, restaurants, period properties and friends' homes

## Why One Chair Beats Three

Owning multiple devices sounds practical until you live with it: transfers between chairs, separate batteries to charge, separate servicing, separate storage. A single all-terrain stair climbing wheelchair means:

- One charger, one warranty, one service plan
- No transfers between chairs throughout the day
- Spontaneity — go anywhere without planning routes around terrain
- Lower lifetime cost than two or three specialist chairs combined

## Comfort for Long Days Out

The X12 Pro Pro upgrade adds **electric legrest adjustment** and a 121° reclining seat with dynamic posture cycling — designed for hours of seated use without fatigue or pressure-sore risk.
