# How to Choose the Right Power Wheelchair for Your Lifestyle

Selecting a power wheelchair is one of the most important decisions you'll make for your mobility and independence. With so many options available, it can feel overwhelming. This guide will help you navigate the key factors to consider.

## Understanding Your Daily Needs

Before looking at specific models, take time to assess your typical day:

- **Where do you spend most of your time?** Indoor use requires different features than outdoor adventures
- **What surfaces do you encounter?** Carpets, gravel, grass, or paved surfaces all demand different capabilities
- **How far do you typically travel?** Battery range matters for longer journeys

## Key Features to Consider

### 1. Seating and Comfort
You'll spend hours in your wheelchair, so comfort is paramount. Look for:
- Adjustable seat depth and width
- Lumbar support options
- Cushioning quality and pressure relief

### 2. Manoeuvrability
Consider the turning radius and overall dimensions. Self-balancing wheelchairs like the XSTO M4 offer exceptional manoeuvrability with their compact design and ability to navigate tight spaces.

### 3. Portability
If you travel frequently, weight and folding capability matter. The XSTO M4 folds in just 5 seconds, making it ideal for car boots and public transport.

### 4. Safety Features
Modern power wheelchairs include:
- Anti-tip technology
- Self-balancing systems
- LED lighting for visibility
- Electromagnetic brakes

## Try Before You Buy

We always recommend booking a demonstration before purchasing. This allows you to:
- Test the controls and responsiveness
- Ensure proper fit and comfort
- Experience features in real-world conditions

Contact your local dealer to arrange a free home demonstration.
