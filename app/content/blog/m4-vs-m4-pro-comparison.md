# XSTO M4 vs M4 Pro: Which Power Wheelchair Is Right for You?

Choosing between the **XSTO M4** and **M4 Pro** usually comes down to capacity, range and comfort features. Both are self-balancing, foldable power wheelchairs from the official UK distributor, Bentech Medical Ltd. Here’s a clear comparison.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | M4 | M4 Pro |
|---------|----|--------|
| Max user capacity | 115 kg | 150 kg |
| Range | 15 km | 26 km |
| Max slope | 10° | 15° |
| Weight (no battery) | 51.5 kg | 60.1 kg |
| Seat height | 347–650 mm | 450–730 mm |
| Seat tilt | — | 0–20° |
| Backrest recline | — | Up to 135° |
| Lighting | — | Integrated LED system |
| Charge time | ~4 hours | ~6 hours |

## Choose the M4 if you want…

- A capable everyday chair with strong portability
- Self-balancing on typical UK slopes up to 10°
- Omnidirectional movement and app/joystick control
- A lower entry price while keeping VAT relief eligibility

The M4 is ideal for users who prioritise folding, boot packing and day-to-day pavement use.

## Choose the M4 Pro if you want…

- Higher weight capacity (150 kg)
- Longer range for bigger days out (26 km)
- Electric seat tilt and deep recline for pressure relief and comfort
- Integrated LED lights for dusk, evenings and roadside visibility
- Standing-desk height seat range (450–730 mm)

## What About the M4B?

The **M4B** sits alongside the M4 with redesigned front wheels and an integrated folding footrest. Capacity and range match the M4; weight is slightly higher at 55.5 kg. Choose M4B if transfers and a tidier folded footprint matter most.
